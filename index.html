Our Mission

Craft that honors the past and holds up for the future.

At Stable Table, we craft timeless pieces and breathe new life into worn or damaged furniture with every cut and restoration. Rooted in craftsmanship and fueled by precision, we specialize in custom woodworking and meticulous restoration projects that honor the past while building unforgettable futures. With a full-service shop and a passion for clean results, we deliver standout work—whether it’s a bespoke creation or a historic revival. Expect top-tier service, free estimates, and responsive support when you need it. Call anytime—Wade Canell and the team are here to make it happen!
